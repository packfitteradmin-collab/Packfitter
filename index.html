About PackFitter

Volume estimates are based on real clothing measurements, standard packing behavior, and a 15% gap factor for dead space inside the bag. Airline carry-on limits are based on published dimensions as of early 2026.

This is a general guide. Final bag acceptance depends on airline discretion and your bag's packed external dimensions.
