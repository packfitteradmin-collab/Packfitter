Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data PackFitter collects, how it's used, and what third-party services are involved. We keep things simple because the site itself is simple.

Data collection

What We Collect

PackFitter does not require accounts, logins, or personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information through the site.

We use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics or similar tools) to understand how visitors use the site. This may include anonymized data such as pages visited, time on site, device type, browser type, and general geographic region.

The site uses cookies for analytics and basic site functionality. These are standard first-party and third-party cookies used by analytics services. No advertising cookies are used.

Usage

How We Use This Data

Analytics data is used solely to understand which pages are visited, how the site is used, and where improvements can be made. We do not sell, share, or distribute any visitor data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Calculator inputs (trip length, climate, packing style, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. No packing data is sent to any server or stored anywhere.

Third parties

Third-Party Services

PackFitter may use the following third-party services:

Analytics: Google Analytics or a similar analytics provider to track anonymized site usage data.

Hosting: The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP address, request time, page requested) as part of normal web hosting operations.

Affiliate links: Some pages may include links to products on Amazon or other retailers. These are affiliate links, meaning PackFitter may earn a commission if you make a purchase. These links do not change the price you pay. Clicking an affiliate link may set a cookie from the retailer to track the referral.

Your rights

Your Rights

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Most browsers also offer "Do Not Track" options. Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal information to request, correct, or delete.

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us through the contact information provided on the site.
